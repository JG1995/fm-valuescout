# FM Moneyball Scouting Tool

This tool is a lightweight, local desktop companion tool to Football Manager players who wish to incorporate the moneyball method of scouting into their game.

Moneyball scouting is a method of scouting that involves finding players that are undervalued by the market, but excel at key metrics that are relevant for their specific role in the game.

The user can use this tool to analyze players in the game world according to certain statistical metrics, either user-defined metrics or ones that come pre-loaded with this app. The user can also observe how the player's metrics have evolved over time in the game-world.

When the user needs to sign a player to fill a role in their squad, the app will analyze all potential signings according to the chosen metrics, and rank them based on percentiles. The app will also suggest a "Top 3" signings - The user can select whether they want to see top 3 by quality, or by value-for-money.

The user can also search for specific players in the database, and see a profile analysis describing how this player would fit into their team, according to their chosen metrics.

The app also saves a running database of the players in the game, allowing the user to scroll through time, seeing how players have evolved throughout their career.
